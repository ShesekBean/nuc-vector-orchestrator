# Product Backlog

## Inbox Triage Agent
- Every morning: surface top 5 emails/messages that need action
- Auto-label each item: reply now, delegate, ignore, wait
- Learn from corrections over time

## Follow-Up Autopilot
- Detect promises and "I'll send..." moments in chat/email
- Auto-create follow-up reminders with due dates
- Nudge when a promise is overdue

## Meeting Intelligence Layer
- Pre-meeting: generate read-ahead + stakeholder brief + likely objections
- Post-meeting: auto-send summary, decisions, owners, deadlines

## Personal Chief-of-Staff Weekly Pack
- One Friday digest: wins, risks, stalled items, next week focus
- Includes "what to stop doing" section

## Decision Journal + Pattern Detector
- Log important decisions and assumptions
- Weekly review: where you were right/wrong and what to update
- Surface recurring patterns and blind spots

## Energy-Aware Planning
- Use sleep/readiness data to dynamically scale the day (deep work vs admin day)
- Prevent overcommitting on low-recovery days
- Integrate with Oura ring data (fitness skill already exists)

## Relationship Maintenance Bot
- Nudge you to ping key people you care about (personal + work)
- Draft warm, contextual check-ins in your voice
- Track last-contact dates, surface people going cold

## Money Autopilot v2
- Alerts for unusual spend, subscription creep, cash thresholds
- Monthly "where money leaked" report + exact fixes
- Extends existing Monarch Money skill (read-only → proactive alerts)
